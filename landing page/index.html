Hand and Paw - web app which makes the world better!

This app is a useful platform for people who care about homeless pets. For those people who want to save animals by taking them from shelters or from hands, and for people who want to save animals by finding a new home for them.

About project

"Hand and Paw" is a final project for HYF web-development course, class 13-14 group 2. This app is created to promote animal adoption in Belgium.
Users are able to find an animal what they want to adopt. They can search shelters by location. All shelters are aggregated in one app. Users can add to favorites animals which they consider to adopt. This app helps shelters to get more attention and also to solve the problem of animals oversupply there. Users can also to register a new shelter as well, if for some reason shelter isn't in the application list.
Users are able to register an animal, which for some reason has lost its owner and needs a new home. A stray pet have a chance to avoid shelter or euthanasia. With help of this app users can adopt animal from hands as well.
"Hand and Paw" is very useful for newcomers to get to know the adoption process in Belgium and what is really unique - the whole app is completely in English.
